CyberVerse is a pixelated, open-world multiplayer game on the Ergo Blockchain. Players can explore, play mini-games, and earn CYPX tokens. By owning a CyberCitizen NFT, players can access Cyberia city and engage in various activities like DJing, car racing, and gardening. The game features a global chat, leaderboards, and loot boxes. To get started, players need to acquire CYPX tokens and obtain a playable character, either by minting or purchasing from the secondary market. The game offers staking rewards, CYPX mining, and mini-games as ways to earn CYPX. CYPX has various in-game utilities, such as acquiring assets, facilitating progression, accessing exclusive drops, and participating in events.
