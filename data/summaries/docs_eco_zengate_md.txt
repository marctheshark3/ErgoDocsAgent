Zengate is a leading blockchain technology company founded in 2022 that builds commercial solutions for governments and businesses. Its ecosystem consists of three core product families: Palmyra ComDEX, Solaris Portal, and Cyberiad. Zengate's mission is to bring financial inclusion for micro, small and medium-sized businesses by leveraging the best features and tools from the extended-UTXO ecosystems such as Ergo and Cardano.
