The document describes the ErgoProfitSharingDapp, a service that allows dapps to distribute gains among their token holders. The first user of this service will be the ErgoMixer, where the creator 'anon2020s' currently receives the mixer's income. The creator plans to create and sell a token called MIX, which can be staked in the Profit Sharing contract to obtain a share of the mixer's income. The document also mentions that the service can be used in the future to provide ERG-staking capabilities.
