This document provides an overview of lending pools in the Ergo ecosystem, a decentralized finance (DeFi) opportunity for earning passive income. It explains how lending pools work, with lenders depositing funds, borrowers taking loans, and interest payments distributed to lenders. The guide covers best practices for participating in lending pools, highlighting the Duckpools platform as an example. It also discusses cross-chain opportunities and the risks associated with lending pools, such as smart contract vulnerabilities and market volatility. The document also introduces SigmaFi, a lending protocol on the Ergo blockchain that facilitates peer-to-peer lending without traditional intermediaries.
