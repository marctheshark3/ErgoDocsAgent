Project Tesseract is an upcoming game being developed by ErgoGames.io, a platform known for integrating blockchain technology and NFTs. The game will feature the collection, breeding, and battling of unique monsters, each represented as an NFT. Key features include open-source development, interoperability with the Ephemeral Bridge for seamless blockchain integration, and Discord integration for player communication. The game is highly anticipated and expected to be released after the launch of Digigoats.
