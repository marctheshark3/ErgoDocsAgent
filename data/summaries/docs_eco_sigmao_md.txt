Sigma'O is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform built on the Ergo blockchain that enables options trading. It allows users to create and trade call and put options on various Ergo EIP-4 tokens. Sigma'O aims to make options trading more accessible and secure by leveraging Ergo's blockchain technology. The platform utilizes Spectrum Liquidity Pools to provide transparent and fair pricing for options. Sigma'O offers features like hedging, speculation, and income generation through options trading. The document provides details on how Sigma'O works, the types of options available, and the benefits of trading options on the platform. It also includes examples of specific options contracts offered on Sigma'O, such as for gold oracle tokens and GORT tokens.
