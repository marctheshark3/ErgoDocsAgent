SentientChain is a blockchain project focused on Non-Interactive Proofs of Proof-of-Work (NIPoPoW) technology. It was initially conceived during the ErgoHack hackathon and is designed as a specialized NIPoPoW sidechain for Play-to-Earn (P2E) games, GameFi (Game Finance), and decentralized exchanges (DEX).
