Mew Finance is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform operating on the Ergo Blockchain, launched in October 2024. It offers a suite of financial applications, including a DEX, marketplace, investment platform, and utilities. Mew Finance utilizes a tiered system to reward user engagement, with higher tiers offering revenue sharing and fee benefits. As of Q4 2024, the MEW token was the 3rd most liquid asset on the Ergo Blockchain. Mew Finance collaborates with other Ergo ecosystem projects and plans to expand its offerings with a lending platform and offer functionality in the marketplace. In Q4 2024, the platform reported total revenue of 2,289.17 ERG, with DEX fees accounting for 76.3% of the total.
