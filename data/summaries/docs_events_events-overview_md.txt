Ergo hosts a variety of events to engage with the community, share updates, and foster innovation. These events include the flagship ERGOHACK and ERGOSUMMIT, which bring together developers, enthusiasts, and thought leaders from the Ergo community. Ergo also hosts weekly Ask Me Anything (AMAs) sessions and regular Developer Updates on Reddit, providing insights into Ergo's development progress and a platform for the community to ask questions directly to the team. To stay updated on all Ergo events, users can join the Ergo Discord community and explore other community spaces.
