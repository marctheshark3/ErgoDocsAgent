ErgOne is an innovative token built on the Ergo blockchain that implements the Proof-of-Commitment Protocol (PoCoP), a unique approach to incentivizing community engagement and ecosystem growth. The PoCoP protocol rewards active participation in the Ergo ecosystem through creating and sharing content, engaging with the community, contributing creative materials, and voting on community content. Users can earn ErgOne tokens based on their Virality Score, which measures their contributions. To get started, users can visit the ErgOne platform, register their Ergo wallet address, and begin submitting content and voting on community contributions.
