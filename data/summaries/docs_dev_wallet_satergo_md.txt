Satergo Wallet is a comprehensive desktop wallet designed for the Ergo ecosystem, featuring an integrated full node for users who wish to support network decentralization or prefer self-reliance. It offers a visual tutorial, full node support, lightweight design, and cross-platform compatibility. Users can connect with Satergo through its website, social media, and the Satergo Offline Vault, an Android application that securely stores encrypted seed phrases and connects with desktops via Bluetooth for transaction safety.
