OptionPools is a decentralized options trading protocol built on the Ergo blockchain, designed to address the limitations of existing DeFi options protocols. It leverages Ergo's unique capabilities, such as its UTXO model and high performance, to create a secure and efficient platform for options trading directly on Layer 1. The protocol aims to bridge the gap between traditional finance and DeFi by offering capital-efficient options trading, overcoming the liquidity challenges and performance bottlenecks faced by current DeFi options protocols. OptionPools includes smart contracts, off-chain code, and a user interface, and introduces innovative features to the DeFi options market.
