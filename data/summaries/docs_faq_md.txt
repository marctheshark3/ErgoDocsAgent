The document provides an overview of the Ergo cryptocurrency platform, including its background, features, and key differences from Bitcoin. Ergo is a resilient platform for contractual money, with a focus on efficiency, security, and ease of use. It uses ErgoScript, a powerful scripting language, to specify conditions for coin usage. Ergo is designed for long-term survivability and self-amendability. The name "Ergo" reflects the platform's logical and work-efficient ethos. Ergo was launched in July 2019, with a pre-launch token called EFYT. The platform has a maximum supply of 97,739,924 Ergs, with a Treasury that receives 4.43% of the released Ergs to fund development and promotion. The Ergo Foundation is a community-driven entity focused on the platform's development and adoption.
