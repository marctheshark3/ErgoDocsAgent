DuckPools is a decentralized finance (DeFi) application built on the Ergo blockchain that offers lending pools for users to lend ERG and its tokens to earn yield and borrow assets. It features algorithmic lending pools and collateralized loans. DuckPools aims to be a catalyst for the Ergo DeFi ecosystem, increasing the utility of ERG and Ergo native assets and boosting the Total Value Locked (TVL) in Ergo DeFi.
