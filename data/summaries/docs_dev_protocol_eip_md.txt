Ergo Improvement Proposals (EIPs) are a set of guidelines and standards designed for the continuous improvement of Ergo, covering core protocol specifications, client APIs, dApp/contract standards, and more. The document outlines several EIPs that have been proposed and implemented, including EIP-0001 (UTXO-Set Scanning Wallet API), EIP-0020 (ErgoPay), EIP-0037 (Tweaking Difficulty Adjustment Algorithm), and EIP-0027 (Emission Retargeting Soft-Fork). The document also provides information on open EIPs, such as EIP-0042 (Multi-Signature Wallet) and EIP-50 (Sigma 6.0), and a hardfork wishlist item related to the limitation of 1 new token per transaction.
