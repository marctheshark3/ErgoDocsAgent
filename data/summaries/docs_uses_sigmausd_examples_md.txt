SigmaRSV is a call option on a percentage of the ERG held in the reserve portion of the SigmaUSD contract. Fees accumulate as users interact with the contract, the reserve amount fluctuates, and the ERG/USD price also fluctuates. SigmaUSD represents a call option on the dollar value of ERG held in reserve, with the price ERG/USD floating as users trade ERG. SigmaUSD is an essential building block for a functioning DeFi system, and the focus for Ergo is now on building use and utility, with the importance of stable value being critical to the long-term prosperity of the Ergo Network.
