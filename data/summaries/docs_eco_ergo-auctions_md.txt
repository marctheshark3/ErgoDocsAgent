Ergo Auction House is the first open-source NFT marketplace on the Ergo blockchain, featuring innovative solutions for efficient smart contracts, various auction types, on-chain collections, and a flexible royalty system. The upcoming Auction House V3 will include a redesigned UI, a verification system to prevent scams, collections utilizing the EIP-34 standard, a launchpad for artists, improved security, new listing types, bulk minting, and flexible royalties. Auction House will also launch its own token, the Auction House Token (AHT), at an upcoming IDO, which will provide exclusive features for contributing artists and NFT buyers.
