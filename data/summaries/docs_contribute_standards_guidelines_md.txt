This document outlines the community guidelines for an online community. The key points are:

- Treat all members with respect and kindness, avoiding hate speech and personal attacks.
- Be open-minded to different perspectives and opinions, and engage constructively with diverse ideas.
- Provide and receive feedback and criticism in a constructive manner, as the community is a place for learning and growth.
- Be thoughtful and substantive in comments, especially on divisive topics.
- Interpret statements charitably and assume good faith.
- Do not engage in spamming, self-promotion, or price manipulation.
- Avoid sharing unverified news or misinformation, and be aware of the risk of scams and hacking.
- Maintain personal and account security, and enjoy being part of the community.
