This document provides guidance for miners on how to withdraw their mining rewards. It covers the different key formats miners may encounter, including base16-encoded raw public keys, Pay-To-Public-Key (P2PK) addresses, and the mining/rewardAddress API method. The document explains the steps to view the mined balance and withdraw the funds, including clearing the node state, restoring the wallet from the miner's seed phrase, checking the balance, and making a transaction to send the rewards to a specified address.
