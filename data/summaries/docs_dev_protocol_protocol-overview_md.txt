Ergo is a next-generation smart contract platform that enables anyone to participate in the digital DeFi revolution. It ensures economic freedom for ordinary people through decentralized, private and secure financial tools. Ergo's core components include Autolykos (ASIC-resistant Proof of Work), eUTXO model, NIPoPoWs (light nodes), privacy features, scaling solutions, storage rent, ErgoScript, and various use cases. The document highlights Ergo's capabilities in powering the future of finance.
