Blobstopia is a game on the Ergo blockchain where players can own blob fighter smart contracts. Players can engage in PVP fights, where the winner takes the bets minus fees. Players can also feed their blobs to increase their attack or defense levels. PVE fights against a Blobinator, a powerful blob fighter, can be initiated by players who have earned enough Spicy Oatmeal Spicy Oatmeal tokens. The original domain for the game is no longer active, but the game has been restored at a new location, allowing users to withdraw their ERGs. While the game is not being actively updated or improved, it remains operational and available for players to enjoy.
