This document explores the role of off-chain components in the Ergo blockchain's decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem. It discusses the distinction between on-chain and off-chain elements, highlighting the importance of off-chain systems like watchers, oracles, and bots in enhancing the capabilities of on-chain processes. The document provides a case study of a transaction bot, details the off-chain services supporting ErgoDex, and explains the role of watchers in the Rosen Bridge. It also covers the requirements and rewards for becoming a watcher or an oracle provider on the Ergo network.
