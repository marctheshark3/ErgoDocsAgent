DigiGoats is a unique and engaging game developed by the Screaming ERGoat Wine Club, where players can feed, train, and battle their Ergoats, with the goal of reaching the top of the leaderboards. Screaming ERGoats are one-of-a-kind NFTs exclusive to the Ergo Blockchain. The third version of DigiGoats, DigiGoats V3, went live at the end of June and can be accessed on the ergoat.com website. The team is now working on their next project, Project Tesseract.
