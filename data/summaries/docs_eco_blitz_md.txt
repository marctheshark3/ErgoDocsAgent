Blitz TCG is an innovative gaming project launched on Ergo that has revolutionized traditional TCG gameplay. It features unique mechanics such as access to the entire deck, a chess-like time bank system, and NPC alternatives on the game board. Blitz TCG is a self-funded project that prioritizes gameplay over profits, providing a play and earn model. The game is available on both the Ergo and Cardano platforms and has successfully launched and is now live. Blitz TCG is designed with competitive players in mind, minimizing the role of luck and emphasizing skill and strategy.
