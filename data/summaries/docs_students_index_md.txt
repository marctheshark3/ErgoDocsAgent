This document provides a comprehensive guide for students interested in learning about the Ergo blockchain and its ecosystem. It covers the intersections between Ergo and various computer science disciplines, including databases, programming languages, networks, systems programming, compiler design, and backend systems. The document also offers resources for getting started with Ergo, such as introductory materials, FAQs, and community engagement opportunities. It then delves into learning ErgoScript, the scripting language used for developing on the Ergo platform, providing a primer, resources for experimentation, and recommended courses. The overall aim is to equip students with the necessary knowledge and tools to explore and contribute to the Ergo ecosystem.
