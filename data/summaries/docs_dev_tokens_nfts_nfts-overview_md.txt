Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) are unique and indivisible blockchain tokens that can represent and prove ownership of digital products. Minting an NFT on Ergo is a straightforward process, and royalties are integrated into the minting metadata. NFTs on Ergo can be used for unforgeable identity, PoW-backed NFTs, and facilitating complex dApps. Key features include the ability to transfer and sell NFTs, manage revenues among multiple token holders, and update or shut down dApps through the NFT.
